How to Give Contributions?

First, thank you for your concern to read documentations before getting started. We hope all informations that we provide now are helping you to understand rAthena as your choice to start your private server. We know that in our current condition, not all matters are mentioned yet to help you in every inch your issue. That is why, we are very welcome for people to help us to improve this documentation project.

It is easy! Just fork us on Github, make a pull request, and we will together review your input.

Besides your contribution for documentation, we also welcome you to join in rAthena Development Team!
